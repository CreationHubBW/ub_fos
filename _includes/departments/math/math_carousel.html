HOD's Welcome

Welcome to the Department of Mathematics. Mathematics is the language of Sciences, but it is equally exciting studied on its own. The modern world is using technology that is Mathematics based. It is therefore imperative to acquire a knowledge of this thread (Mathematics) that binds Sciences, Technology and Engineering. The financial sector of the country is also growing and requires Mathematical skills to understand, predict and unravel this new exciting frontier. The Department therefore offers specializations in areas across the broad spectrum of Mathematics at both undergraduate and postgraduate level. On behalf of the Department, the Faculty and indeed the University join us and be part of this exciting team either as a student or staff member.
